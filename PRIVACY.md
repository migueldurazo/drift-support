# Drift Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** [Month Day, Year]

Drift ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Drift handles data.

## 1. Data We Use

Drift uses:

- **Microphone audio** (with your permission)
- **Speech recognition** (with your permission)

This is used only to power in-app features like phrase detection, sound effects, and background music selection.

## 2. Audio and Speech Handling

Drift listens while you actively use the listening feature.

- We do **not** create user accounts.
- We do **not** store raw recordings in app storage for later playback.
- We do **not** sell your personal data.

## 3. Data Collection and Tracking

Drift does not use advertising SDKs and does not serve ads.

If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated before rollout.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information.

We only share data when legally required or to protect rights and safety.

## 5. Children’s Privacy

Drift is designed for family bedtime use.  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 6. Data Retention

Drift is designed to process listening data during use.  
No persistent user profile is required for core functionality.

## 7. Your Choices

You can control permissions at any time in iOS Settings:

- Microphone
- Speech Recognition

If you disable these permissions, listening-based features may not work.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
